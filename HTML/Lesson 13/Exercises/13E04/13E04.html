Brooklyn Nine-Nine
Cast:
  • Andy Samberg as Jake Peralta
  • Melissa Fumero as Amy Santiago
  • Stephanie Beatriz as Rosa Dias
  • Joe Lo Truglio as Charles Boyle
  • Terry Crews as Terry Jeffords

Jake Peralta, a talented detective, along with his diverse yet carefree team, struggles when a new commanding officer, Ray Holt, is appointed to their precinct.
