At East High School, "High School Musical; The Musical The Series" introduces members of the drama club and their faculty advisors as they work their way toward the opening night of their school's first-ever production of "High School Musical: The Musical." New romances blossom, old friendships are tested to the limit while new ones are formed, rivalires flare, and lives are changed forever as these students discover the [illegible] power that only their high school can provide.
