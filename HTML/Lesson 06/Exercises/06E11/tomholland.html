Tom Holland

Thomas Stanley Holland (born 1 June 1996) is an English actor.
His accolades include a British Academy Film Award, a Guinness World Record and an appearance on the Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe list.
Some publications have called him one of the best actors under 30 years of age.

He is best known for his role as Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, although he stars in many other movies such as Uncharted, Cherry.
He has also played voice roles in Spies in Disguise, Onward, and Dolittle.

In his career, he has quickly made a name for himself, and also has a reputation of spoling the marvel movies he's in.
He's done it enough times that he's no longer given the full move script in efforts to keep him from giving away the plot.
