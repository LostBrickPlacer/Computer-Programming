Jim Carrey


Jim Carrey, in full James Eugene Carrey, (born January 17, 1962, New Market, Ontario, Canada).
A Canadian American comedian who established himself as a leading comedic actor with a series of over-the-top performances and who won plaudits for his more-serious portrayals as his career progressed. Some movies he's been in include Sonic The Hedgehog, Bruce Almighty, Yes Man, and many more.
