Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

Dwayne Douglas Johnson (born May 2, 1972), also known by his ring name The Rock, is an American actor, businessman, and former professional wrestler.
Widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time, he wrestled for WWE for eight years prior to pursuing an acting career.
His films have grossed over $3.5 billion in North America and over $10.5 billion worldwide, making him one of the world's highest-grossing and highest-paid actors.

He makes over $40 000 000 USD per year, and stars in over 50 different movies.
Even with his acting career, he has 3 children, named Simone, Jasmine, and Tiana, who are 20 years old, 6 years old, and 3 years old, respectively.
