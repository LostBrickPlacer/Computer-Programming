Great Chemistry Ideas

Chemistry can be fun! Here are some experiments you can do at home.

  • Try mixing baking soda with vinegar! The results will suprise you!
  • Also try throwing a chunk of sodium into a lake! The results might blow your mind!
  • Another classic, try splitting a uranium atom in two! The results are killer!
