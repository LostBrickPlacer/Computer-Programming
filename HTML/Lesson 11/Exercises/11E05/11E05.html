MANGO PUDDING RECIPE!!

Step 1:
Get Three potatoes, a container of sour cream, and a packet of salt. Then put them on a fancy looking piece of wood.
Step 2:
Fill a green bucket with 3 cups of bleach. Then use a spatula and smear it around the edges of the bucket. Make sure to get your finger in the image when you take the picture.
Step 3:
Put flour into the bucket that has bleach in it until it all turns white. Mix it until you see lots of bubbles. Then break some eggs and take only the yolks (leave the egg whites, you don't need them) and put them into the center of the bucket.
Step 4:
Mash some mango into the bucket and mix it around with the spatula until it looks goopy. Pour 3 cups of sugar in, then continue mixing while you add one tablespoon of powdered mango.
Step 5:
Repeat steps 1-4 five times, each in a very nice looking white bowl. Fill one of the bowls with laxatives, mix them around and let sit on a table in the sun for at least 6 weeks.
Step 6:
Buy an antique ceramic spoon and some mint leaves to garnish your mango sludge. Cut up some extra mango and sprinkle it in the corner of the bowl. Take a picture and post it on the internet for internet points.

Enjoy!
