life guards shared 10 facts to help you stay safe in the water

  1. When lifeguards ask you not to run, they mean it.
  2. It's prohibited to dig deep holes in the sand for a reason.
  3. Holding your breath is not a game.
  4. Beware of sea creatures.
  5. Stay where lifeguards can see you. If you can't see them, they can't see you.
  6. Stay away from piers.
  7. If you are in a rip current, swim parallel to the beach.
  8. Wakling barefoot is a bad idea.
  9. Swim only when you're sober.
  10. Stop talking to lifeguards without a good reason. It is distracting.
