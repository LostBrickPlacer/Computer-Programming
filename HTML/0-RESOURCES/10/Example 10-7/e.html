Devil Summoner- Raidou Kuzunoha vs The Soullees Army (2004): Is an ARPG instead of Jrpg like others so the mechanic are dompletely different (besides demon fusion). Story is mediorce and can get repetitive. The titular character is cool though. 2/5

Devil Summoner- Raidou Kuzunoha vs King Abbaddon (2006) : Has better mechanic but suffers from even worse plot. 2/5

Devil Survivor 1(2007) and Devil Survivor Overclocked (2009: An awesome game that incoporates the best from both Persona and SMT. Has great story, gameplay and characters. Also is easy to access. 5/5

Devil Survivor 2(2009) and Devil Survivor Record Breaker (2012) : Same thing as 1 but the story is a bit inferior. Still an awesome game. 5/5
