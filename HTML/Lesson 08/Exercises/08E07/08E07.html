The Score is an American alternative rock band formed in New York City in 2011.
The group consists of Eddie Anthony Ramirez (lead vocals, guitar)
and Edan Chai Dover (backing vocals, keys, producer). They were signed to Republic
Records in 2015 after their song "Oh My Love" was featured in an advertising campaign for Asda supermarkets.
