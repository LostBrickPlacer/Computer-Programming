CANADA

Canada is a country in the northern part of North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic to the Pacific and northward into the Arctic Ocean, covering 9.98 million (9 980 000) square kilometers (3.86 million square miles), making it the world's second-largest country by total area. Its southern border with the United States, streching some 8,891 kilometers (5,525 miles), is the world's longest bi-national border. Canada's capital is Ottawa, and its three largest metropolitan areas are Toronto, Montreal and Vancouver.
