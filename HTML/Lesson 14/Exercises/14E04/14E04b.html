Atelier Arland Bundle - Rorona, Meruru, Totori Review











































Fill the land of Arland with the beauty of alchemist!-Nintendo Switch Online


Metacritic: 76%

Nintendo Life: 7/10

This is a series I did not want to pick up due to how "sugary" it looks. And I was kind of right: this series is full of light-hearted fluff. Don't come to this series expecting action packed games. This bundle came with 3 games from the 3DS: Rorona, Totori, Meruru. Atelier mainly focuses on crafting, gathering and has some combat segments. It's kind of like Rune Factory 4 but instead of farm-sim, it's crafting. The art really showed its age. As expected, the company did not polish the art too much before porting it. The story is for casual and relaxing but can be borderline esoterically happy. The combat in the game is very simple and should not trouble you too much. The biggest flaw of the bundle is how much potential the plotline could have gone for the better but did not. Ranking game wise: Rorona > Meru > Totori. 6/10 for me!
